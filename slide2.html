GIT is the largest immune organ in the body, and it is the main cause to switch formula during the 1st year of life1,2
FGIDs are extremely common, affecting nearly half of the infants around the globe.3
In Saudi Arabia, 40% had tried at least 2 different formula brands in the first year of life.2
GIT: Gastrointestinal tract; FGIDs: Functional gastrointestinal disorders.
