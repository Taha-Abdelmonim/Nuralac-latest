Human milk oligosaccharides are the third most abundant component of breast milk1
Studies showed that HMOs have several
health benefits:
Modulation of intestinal microbiota.1
Development of the immune system.1
Anti-adhesive effect against pathogens.1
Modulation of intestinal epithelial cell response.1
HMOs: Human milk oligosaccharides; 2’-FL: 2’Fucosyllactose.
1. Hegar B, Wibowo Y, Basrowi RW, et al. The Role of Two Human Milk Oligosaccharides, 2′-Fucosyllactose and Lacto-N-Neotetraose, in Infant Nutrition. Pediatr Gastroenterol Hepatol Nutr. 2019;22(4):330-340.
